★ My recommendation: A (Focus Mode) as the spine + D (Compact Triage) as an optional "list view"

Focus Mode delivers the calm, "one calm card, <60s, no fear" defining experience for the typical owner with a handful of findings; a toggle to the Compact Triage list serves the rare power user with many findings. Both reuse the same FindingCard atoms, ActionBar, snippet, and the in-line document viewer from B. Card-stack swipe (C) is a post-MVP delight layer; the Wizard (E) is the fallback if usability testing shows anxiety. Tell me your pick (or a different combination).

Mockups are static HTML for direction selection — not production components. Palette, type, and reversible-action principles are applied from the UX spec's Visual Foundation.
